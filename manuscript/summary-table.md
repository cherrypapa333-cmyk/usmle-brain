# Summary Table: Music Therapy in Heart Failure (Updated)

| Study | Design & Population | Intervention | Outcomes Measured | Key Findings | Mechanistic Domain (PNIE) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Yu et al. (2025)** | Single-arm pilot, 20 HF/COPD patients post-discharge | 2 in-person + 2 virtual hybrid music therapy sessions | Feasibility, engagement, anxiety, depressive symptoms | Feasible and acceptable (~85% retention); barriers around technology use and post-discharge communication | Neural / Behavioral |
| **Hosseini et al. (2024)** | RCT, 76 hospitalized HF patients | 30 min/night nature music for 3 nights via headphones | Sleep quality (Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index), daily function, sedative use | Significant improvement in sleep quality (P<0.001) and daily function (P=0.002); reduced sedative use (P<0.001) | Neural / Entrainment |
| **Burrai et al. (2020)** | RCT, 159 HF patients, home-care setting | 30 min/day classical music for 3 months via MP3 player | HF-specific & generic QOL, sleep, anxiety, depression, cognition | Significant improvement across all domains (QOL P<0.001; sleep P=0.007; anxiety/depression P<0.001; cognition P=0.003) | Neural / Endocrine / Cognitive |
| **Burrai et al. (2018)** | Conceptual review and framework | PNIE framework for music in HF | Physiological & psychological pathways (HR, BP, cytokines) | Proposed PNIE model linking neural, endocrine, and immune effects | Framework (all axes) |
| **Najafi Ghezelejh et al. (2016)** | Quasi-experimental, 90 HF inpatients (control vs. massage vs. music) | 3 × 20 min sessions of patient-preferred music vs. back massage | Fatigue scores pre/post intervention | Music significantly reduced fatigue; massage produced larger effect — supports multimodal use | Neural / Endocrine |
| **Okada et al. (2009)** | Controlled trial, 87 elderly CVD patients (CHF subset) | 10 × 45 min music therapy sessions | HRV, IL-6, adrenaline, noradrenaline, CHF events | Increased parasympathetic activity, reduced cytokines and catecholamines, fewer CHF events | Neural / Endocrine / Immune |

## Abbreviations

- **HF** — Heart Failure
- **CHF** — Congestive Heart Failure
- **QOL** — Quality of Life
- **RCT** — Randomized Controlled Trial
- **COPD** — Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease
- **PNIE** — Psychoneuroimmunoendocrinological
- **HR** — Heart Rate
- **BP** — Blood Pressure
- **HRV** — Heart Rate Variability
- **IL-6** — Interleukin-6
- **LF/HF** — Low-frequency to high-frequency ratio (HRV)

## What was added vs. the original table

- Added "sedative use" to Hosseini et al. outcomes (a clinically important secondary endpoint).
